TurboDictate

Voice to Text Made Easy

💻 Windows 📱 Android

TurboDictate is a fast, private voice-to-text application powered by Groq's Whisper AI. It transforms your speech into text in seconds, working anywhere on your device. On Windows, use global hotkeys to dictate directly into any application. On Android, use the custom voice keyboard to type by speaking in any text field. Supports both English and Arabic transcription.
